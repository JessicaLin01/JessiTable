About Us

Jessi Table was established in 2017. The founders are two students who studys in National Taipei University of Education.
With the enthusiasm and the love for coffee,
we want to give the best to our customers.
We choose the high-quality coffee beans and the carefully selected natural ingredients to ensure our quality.

Concept

Due to our love for coffee, we want to let more and more people to enjoy the leisure time with a cup of coffee.

Environment

We provide a place that you can feel relax and stress-free, and you can enjoy your time alone or with friends and family.

Ingredient

We use the local ingredients and high quality beans from other countries, hope every customers can enjoy the best taste of coffee.
